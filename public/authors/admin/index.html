Hello, I’m Kevin Xu ZHONG (钟旭), currently a research associate working in The Suttle Lab at The University of British Columbia. I love studying viruses of all kinds (dsDNA, RNA, ssDNA viruses) and from different environments (lakes, oceans, deepsea, sediments, etc.) and hosts (microbes, sea mammals, sea foods, etc.). I specialize in molecular biology and bioinformatics for omics analysis. I’m a big fun of R and make new packages. I love to develope methods for moving forward our understanding about viruses, microbes, and ecosystems in following fields:

  • Environmental virology

  • Environmental microbiology

  • Host-associated microbiome

  • Or maybe Marine science
